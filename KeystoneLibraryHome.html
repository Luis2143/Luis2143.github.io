KEYSTONE LIBRARY

We here at the Olympians have been tasked with designing a library which will also serve as a museum with an aspirational/historical theme. We dont want this library to turn into a holding area for old books and records. Showcasing
new technologies and including everyone is our main goal here at the Olympians.

Why us?

We want to combine the wonders of Ancient Rome and Greece with our modern world and advancing technologies. Hate has no place here at the Library, we've noticed that hate is becoming more and more prevalent in our daily lives and the media. This is why we have designed the library around making everyone and anyone feel included and wanted.

Upcoming Technology

We're in the 21st centuary. Why not showcase some of this time-period's most facinating technology? We have Modern Computers capable of running industy-grade CAD software, 5G servers for the fastest connection, and Stereolithographic 3D printers, all free of use for any UNCC students.

Greek Architecture

Ancient Greece was a time of political, philosophical, artistic, and scientific achievements. We'd like to combine this era of progress with our own to create a library that has the ability to immerse people in an accurate renditioning of Ancient Greece.

Study Areas

Students aslike need a quiet and peaceful area where they can study in peace, The library offers 5 different areas designated solely to this. Each area is different from one another, we want each area to have its own unique feeling.

Social Impact

The impact is how mythology helps with bringing order to the world and an understanding of certain aspects that can't be properly explained, This also allows people to think about the past of humankind and what it has in common with modern humankind.

Enviormental Impact

The Olympians have had experience with designing and building sustainable homes before. Incorporating our prior knowlege and green building pracices we've been able to create a near zero waste Library and plan on going carbon netural in the near future.
